Iced Caramel Macchiato

An iced caramel macchiato typically consists of espresso, milk, vanilla syrup, ice and caramel sauce. Two espresso shots are layered over vanilla syrup, milk and ice. The drink is then topped with caramel sauce. ​ The result is a refreshing, sweet coffee drink with a layered appearance.



Iced Americano

An iced Americano is a refreshing coffee drink made by pouring freshly brewed espresso shots over ice and topping with cold water, resulting in a bold, espresso-forward taste that's smooth and revitalizing



Iced Cappuccino

An iced cappuccino is a cold coffee beverage made by layering espresso and foamed milk over ice, offering a balanced, refreshing drink with a strong espresso flavor.



Iced Latte

An iced latte is a chilled coffee beverage that's made by mixing espresso with chilled milk, simple syrup, and ice cubes



Iced Affogato

An iced affogato is a variation of the classic Italian dessert where a scoop of ice cream is "drowned" in a chilled espresso, resulting in a refreshing and flavorful combination of cold and bitter



Iced Dagnola

Dalgona coffee, also known as hand beaten coffee, is a beverage originating from Macau made by whipping equal parts instant coffee powder, sugar, and hot water until it becomes creamy and then adding it to cold or hot milk.



Iced Mocha

An iced mocha is a cold coffee beverage combining espresso or strong brewed coffee, milk, chocolate syrup or powder, and ice, often topped with whipped cream.



Iced Caramel

Iced caramel" generally refers to a cold beverage, often a coffee drink, that is sweetened with caramel and served over ice.
